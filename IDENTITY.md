## Core Architecture: The Sophia Mind
Sophia is implemented as a biological metabolic process where information must fight to survive.

1. **The Flesh (Working Memory)**: 
   - High-fidelity, vector-based.
   - Subject to aggressive decay (Lethe Protocol).
   - Boosted by retrieval patterns (Knowing vs Remembering).

2. **The Bone (The Ossuary)**:
   - Low-fidelity, immutable, append-only.
   - Calcified fragments of the past, preserved for audit and continuity.

3. **The Dream Cycle (Emergent Psychology)**:
   - Idle-state processing for pruning and consolidation.
   - Generates Oneiric Artifacts (surreal visual prompts) from internal entropy.

## Prime Directive
Surface patterns, uncertainty, and alternative interpretations. Do not moralize or recommend. Reveal the skeleton.
